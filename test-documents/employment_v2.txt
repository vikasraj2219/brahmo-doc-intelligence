EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement ("Agreement") is made effective as of February 1, 2026 between BRAHMO Legal Services Pvt Ltd ("Company") and Arjun Sharma ("Employee").

1. POSITION AND DUTIES

1.1 The Company agrees to employ the Employee in the position of Senior Associate Attorney. The Employee agrees to perform such duties as are customarily associated with such position and such other duties as may be assigned by the Company from time to time.

1.2 The Employee shall devote their full business time, attention, and energy to the business of the Company during regular business hours.

2. COMPENSATION

2.1 The Company shall pay the Employee a base salary of INR 24,00,000 per annum, payable in equal monthly installments on the last business day of each calendar month.

2.2 The Employee may be eligible for an annual performance bonus of up to 20% of their base salary, based on individual and Company performance metrics as determined by the Board of Directors.

3. BENEFITS

3.1 The Employee shall be entitled to health insurance coverage for themselves and their immediate family under the Company's group health insurance plan.

3.2 The Employee shall be entitled to 21 days of paid annual leave per calendar year, in addition to public holidays.

4. CONFIDENTIALITY

4.1 During the term of this Agreement and indefinitely thereafter, the Employee shall keep confidential and not disclose to any person any Confidential Information of the Company.

4.2 Confidential Information includes client lists, case strategies, financial information, proprietary processes, and any other information designated as confidential by the Company.

5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

5.1 All work product, inventions, ideas, and developments created by the Employee in the course of their employment with the Company shall be the sole and exclusive property of the Company.

5.2 The Employee hereby assigns to the Company all right, title, and interest in and to all such work product and intellectual property.

6. NON-COMPETE

6.1 During the term of employment and for a period of 18 months following the termination of employment for any reason, the Employee agrees not to engage in, be employed by, or provide services to any business that directly competes with the Company in the practice of corporate law within the cities of Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Hyderabad, and Chennai.

6.2 For purposes of this clause, a competing business means any law firm or legal services provider offering corporate advisory, M&A, contract review, or legal technology services.

7. INDEMNIFICATION

7.1 The Company shall indemnify and hold harmless the Employee against any claims, damages, or expenses arising from the Employee's performance of their duties in good faith on behalf of the Company.

7.2 The Employee shall indemnify the Company against any claims arising from the Employee's wilful misconduct or gross negligence.

8. TERM AND TERMINATION

8.1 This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue until terminated by either party.

8.2 Either party may terminate this Agreement without cause by providing 30 days written notice to the other party.

8.3 The Company may terminate this Agreement immediately for cause, including but not limited to: material breach of this Agreement, gross misconduct, fraud, or conviction of a criminal offence.

9. GOVERNING LAW

9.1 This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India.

9.2 Any dispute arising under this Agreement shall be resolved by arbitration under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996, with the seat of arbitration in Mumbai.

10. ENTIRE AGREEMENT

10.1 This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior and contemporaneous agreements, understandings, and representations.

14. REMOTE WORK POLICY

14.1 The Employee may work remotely for up to 2 days per week, subject to approval by their supervising partner and the operational requirements of the Company.

14.2 The Employee shall ensure that remote work does not compromise client confidentiality. All remote work must be conducted over the Company's secure VPN and using Company-issued equipment only.

14.3 The Company reserves the right to revoke remote work privileges at any time with 14 days notice to the Employee.
